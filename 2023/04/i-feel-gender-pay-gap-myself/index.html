I feel Gender Pay Gap myself

April 12, 2023 in Gender pay

  • Ask your friends’ salary

Ask your friends’ salary

It was a very nice dinner with my university friends. I have contact to only few of them. They were among them. My friend X quit his job in one start-up company and is looking for new opportunities. He told me that he already get an offer which is xx euro. The other friend A recently moved to other city from berlin. I knew his salary which was better after his previous company which laid him off. That makes me think, why I work so hard and own still less than my uni friends. There are individual factors like previous working experience. Structural factors exist in the workplace. According to the recent statistics, the gender pay gap in Germany is around 18%, indicating that women earn 18% less than men in similar positions.

Ksenia Legostay organized the annual anonymous IT salary survey for some years. From her report in 2020, the difference in median salaries of men and women is also around 18%. It’s a pity that they didn’t have data of gender in 2021 and 2022.

https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7025950183557361664/

According to the collected data, the difference in median salaries of men and women is around 18%. You can find out more about the pay gap in a survey by the Statistisches Bundesamt based on the employees’ payrolls of approximately 60,000 companies randomly selected across Germany. They estimated the average pay gap to be at 19%.
