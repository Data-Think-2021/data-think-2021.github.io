I started this blog at the end of last December. What an year! Recently I even became a proud mom of a samoyed dog. The life is full of adventure, love and suprise.

🚀 Personal Highlights

  • Lauched a blog website but had no time to continue updating contents after the first half year
  • Be a proud mom of two happy school children, two cats and one dog.
  • Family visit in China after six long years.
  • Yoga session for one year
  • Started investing in fonds and stocks
  • Currently attend online and individual German courses to refine my German

📊 Data Journey and Career

  • Completed Data Engineering Zoomcamp from DataTalks.Club
  • Completed MLOps Zoomcamp from DataTalks.Club
  • Earned AWS Certified Solution Architect
  • Worked in BIG4 and gained great experience in consultancy
  • Transitioned to the public sector to help build the German charging infrasture with a focus on robust data foundations
  • Mentored and supported the growth of 1 junior data professional
  • Sponsored the DataTalks.Club community from my learning budget

🎊 New Year Resolution

  • Be a supportive mom
  • Say NO and focus on the most important things
  • Improve German proficiency to communicate and write confidently
  • Start a social project to help women/moms with migrant background to find jobs
  • Start a side project, freelancing and take the challenges

Die Zeit vergeht wie im Flug. 2023 ist bald vorbei. Was habe ich geschafft und nicht geschafft? Ich versuche jetzt immer auf Deutsch zu schreiben, weil Deutsch sehr wichtig auf meiner Arbeit ist. Ich bin vor einem Monat Hundmama geworden und habe einen wunderschönen Samojede Hund, der sich ,Happy’ heißt.
