About me

Hello, I’m Xia, and I bring a diverse background and a wealth of experience to the dynamic world of data science.

Armed with a Master’s degree in Automotive Engineering, I embarked on a transformative journey by earning a second Master’s in Data Science. This unique blend of engineering and data expertise has equipped me with a holistic understanding of complex systems.

My professional journey has been marked by significant experiences in various sectors. I’ve contributed to impactful projects in a federal research institute, a small consultancy, and a Big 4 consultancy. Currently, I hold a pivotal role, overseeing data infrastructure in Germany.

My technical prowess encompasses proficiency in Python, coupled with the distinction of being a certified AWS Solutions Architect. I specialize in ETL processes, machine learning (ML), natural language processing (NLP), and comprehensive data analysis.

Beyond technical proficiency, my success is rooted in a set of essential soft skills. I pride myself on my problem-solving abilities, keen business understanding, unwavering reliability, and effective communication.

Fueling my journey is an unbridled passion for data science. I am driven by the belief that effective data utilization can bring about transformative change. My approach is client-centric, and I am committed to delivering solutions tailored to unique business needs.

Whether you’re navigating the complexities of data infrastructure or seeking innovative data science solutions, I am here to collaborate and bring your vision to life. Let’s embark on this data-driven journey together.
